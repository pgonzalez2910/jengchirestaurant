Moon Festival

Mid-Autumn Festival, mooncakes, family unity, and seasonal bakery traditions.

Moon Festival

Mid-Autumn Festival & Mooncakes

The Mid-Autumn Festival is a harvest festival held on the 15th day of the eighth month in the Chinese calendar, during a full moon. Making and sharing mooncakes is one of the hallmark traditions.

In Chinese culture, a round shape symbolizes completeness and unity. Sharing round mooncakes among family members represents family unity.
