POSITION DESCRIPTION: SERVER

Job ID: JAF-SVR-001 | Department: Front of House | Reports To: Restaurant Manager

Position Summary

The Server provides exceptional guest service by taking orders, delivering food and beverages, and ensuring a positive dining experience. This role requires frequent movement, lifting, carrying, and interaction in a fast-paced restaurant environment. All essential functions are performed with or without reasonable accommodation under the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).

Essential Functions

Function Frequency Physical Demand ADA Accommodation Notes
Greet and seat guestsConstantLightVerbal communication; mobility to host stand
Take food/beverage ordersConstantLightMemory, verbal/written communication
Carry trays (up to 25 lbs)FrequentMediumMay use tray jack or assistance as accommodation
Navigate dining roomConstantLightReasonable path modifications considered
Process payments via POSFrequentSedentaryVisual/accommodation for screen access
Communicate with kitchenConstantLightVerbal, written, or assistive tech acceptable

Physical Requirements

  • Stand/Walk: 90% of shift (up to 8 hours)
  • Lift/Carry: Up to 25 lbs frequently; 50 lbs occasionally
  • Movement: Frequent bending, kneeling, reaching
  • Environment: Hot kitchen proximity, wet floors, noise levels 70-85 dB

Minimum Qualifications

  • Age 18+ (or per state law)
  • Ability to read, write, and communicate in English
  • Prior serving experience preferred; training provided
  • Ability to work in team environment under time pressure
  • Food Handler Certification (obtained within 30 days of hire)

ADA Compliance Statement

Jeng Chi Restaurant is an equal opportunity employer. We provide reasonable accommodations to qualified individuals with disabilities, provided the accommodation does not impose an undue hardship on business operations. Employees requesting accommodation should contact HR using the ADA Accommodation Request Form. All medical information is kept confidential in a separate file.
