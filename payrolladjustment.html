Payroll Compliance Disclaimer — TWC / DOL / EEOC: This Payroll Adjustment & Corrections Log is an internal administrative payroll control document. All earnings and deductions must be reviewed for accuracy, consistency, and lawful payroll treatment before payroll is finalized. Dollar entries should be reviewed as monetary payroll adjustments. Hour entries should be reviewed against timekeeping records, approved schedules, PTO records, or manager approval. Earnings such as PTO hours, large party payments, reimbursements, retroactive pay, missing hours, overtime corrections, bonuses, and pay-rate corrections should be supported by approved schedules, timekeeping records, manager approvals, payroll records, or other business documentation. Deductions such as salary advance repayment, uniform deductions, overpayment repayment, meal deductions, court-ordered deductions, tax withholdings, or other non-required deductions should be supported by written employee authorization when required and should clearly identify the amount, purpose, and payroll treatment of the deduction. This log should be applied consistently and without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, pregnancy, retaliation status, or any other protected category. This document does not replace official payroll records, wage notices, tax documents, employee authorizations, legal review, or payroll processor review. Final payroll determinations remain subject to HR, management, ownership, payroll processor, Texas Workforce Commission requirements, U.S. Department of Labor/FLSA requirements, EEOC requirements, and applicable federal/state wage-and-hour requirements.
